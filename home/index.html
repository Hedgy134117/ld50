TASK CHAMPION

HOW TO PLAY

the goal of the game is to complete as many tasks as possible, delaying the inevitability of the timer running out

there are four different types of tasks

CLICKER TASK

arguably the easiest task, all you need to do is click the button the amount of times required.

ORDER TASK

click the buttons in ascending order. if you click the wrong one, this task will penalize you by adding 1 second to the timer.

MATH TASK

answer the math question by clicking the correct button. if you click the wrong one, this task will penalize you by adding 1 second to the timer.

TEXT TASK

arguably the hardest task, all you need to do is copy the sentence into the text box.

SCORING

for every task the is on the screen, and for every 1/10th of a second that passes, your score goes up by one

for example, there's 3 tasks on screen, in one second you get 3 * 10 = 30 points

you get an additional 100 points every time you complete a task

that's about all there is too it. good luck out there, and become the next TASK CHAMPION!
